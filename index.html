FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What age children do you accept? ▼

We accept children aged 3 to 5 years old for academic years and up to 6 years old for our summer camp programs. Children must be potty trained prior to attendance.

Do you follow the local school district schedule? ▼

Yes, Patchwork Preschool generally follows the Provo City School District calendar for holidays, breaks, and professional development days, making scheduling easier for local families.

What is your teaching philosophy? ▼

Our philosophy is rooted in traditional preschool structures where learning is active and hands-on. We focus on academic fundamentals like literacy and math while prioritizing social-emotional skills through intentional, joyful interaction.
